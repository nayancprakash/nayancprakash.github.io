Space Exploration Alberta Robotics

Space Exploration Alberta Robotics, or SPEAR, is a student design club at the University of Alberta aiming to build a Mars rover. Being a member of SPEAR may involve the design, use and/or implementation of various hardware, software, mechanical, and electrical systems. Competing each year against other schools' Mars rovers, this club must also maintain its own finances as well as market itself to additional sponsors.

This year, as a member of the AI team, I will be assisting in developing a Computer Vision system that will allow the Mars rover, TARS, to autonomously navigate its way through unwieldy terrain.

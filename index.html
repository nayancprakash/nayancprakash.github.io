Census is an area-based polling app currently being developed by myself and two other Software Engineering Students, Ryan Shukla and Mitch Adam. Census is designed to be enjoyable, informative and useful to individuals, businesses and governments alike.

Census is currently being developed using the React Native app development framework, as well as Node.js as a backend engine which interfaces with a MYSQL Database.

I have taken on the role of a full-stack engineer writing code for both the Frontend and the Backend.

Space Exploration Alberta Robotics, or SPEAR, is a student design club at the University of Alberta aiming to build a Mars rover. Being a member of SPEAR may involve the design, use and/or implementation of various hardware, software, mechanical, and electrical systems. Competing each year against other schools' Mars rovers, this club must also maintain its own finances as well as market itself to additional sponsors.

This year, as a member of the AI team, I will be assisting in developing a Computer Vision system that will allow the Mars rover, TARS, to autonomously navigate its way through unwieldy terrain. Specifically, this is being done by the using the OpenCV Library and C++ to use computer vision and detect objects. This object detection must communicate with TARS' other systems and this is done through the Robot Operating System.

A* Battles is a two-player capture the flag game that utilizes the A* Pathfinding algorithm. Play locally or online!

Developed by Mitch Adam and myself, A* Battles was developed in Python and utilizes both Tornado as a framework and WebSockets as a protocol for online multiplayer.

Starting in high school, I sought out more and more about software, including taking Computer Science courses, building a computer, and programming in multiple languages. I am working to release a mobile app. I have joined clubs that merge all of my interests into one setting. I am highly motivated and love to apply my interest and skill in sciences, creativity, programming, computer sciences, and electronics. I am constantly working to collaborate and work with others and always push to learn more and improve myself.
